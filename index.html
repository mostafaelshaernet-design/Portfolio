Multi-Area OSPF Design

Enterprise Multi-Area OSPF with RIP Redistribution

Built a multi-area OSPF backbone in GNS3 spanning Area 0, Area 20, Area 40, and Area 80, linked through a central ISP edge router to two external RIPv2 autonomous systems. Implemented route redistribution between the OSPF and RIP domains and validated reachability using loopback interfaces across every area.

GNS3 OSPF Multi-Area RIPv2 Route Redistribution
